About Me

I embarked on my academic journey with the dream of building robots, and initially, I believed that electronics would be the key field for me.

However, as I delved deeper into the intricacies of robotics, I realized that creating a truly remarkable and intelligent robot requires collaboration across various disciplines.

This revelation led me to explore the realm of software engineering, which surprisingly offered the perfect platform to pursue my robotic aspirations.

In the world of software engineering, I discovered the thrilling opportunity to craft the very essence of a robot‚Äîthe artificial intelligence that serves as its brain. For me, this aspect proved to be the most captivating and engaging part of the entire process. Although I had initially focused on electronics, my curiosity gradually expanded to encompass diverse areas within computer science. Two particular domains caught my attention: cybersecurity, ignited by an intriguing book I read, and game programming, a long-held childhood dream.

At present, my greatest passion lies in the vast field of artificial intelligence. I find immense joy in exploring its multifaceted forms, whether it be designing AI systems for video games, creating intelligent bots, or delving into the fascinating realm of deep learning. Nevertheless, I continue to keep an eye on the latest developments in cybersecurity vulnerabilities by regularly perusing exploitdb, as I believe in constantly expanding my knowledge base.

By channeling my enthusiasm into AI and embracing its myriad applications, I strive to bring forth innovative solutions and push the boundaries of what is possible in this ever-evolving field.
